RULES TO PLAY

How to win

The player who will score a total of 50 first will win the Game.

How to play

You have to roll the dice to get a number and the numbers will keep on adding in your total score to reach a total of 50 and the game ends.

Use of hold button

If 1 comes up when you roll the dice then all your current score will be zero. So to avoid the situation where you have made a good current score and you want to add it in your total score then you can use hold button to add it and pass the chance to the opponent.
